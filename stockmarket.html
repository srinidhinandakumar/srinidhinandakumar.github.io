Project Stock Market Prediction

I developed a prediction technique to predict closing stock prices given opening prices.

STOCK MARKET PREDICTION

This project employed the application of neural networks. I used the Levenberg-Marquardt algorithm for non-linear data fitting problems using the MATLAB inbuilt library NNtoolbox, for training purposes.

The accuracy observed in the results was remarkable.
